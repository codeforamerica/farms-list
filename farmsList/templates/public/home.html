About the Urban Farming Program

The properties listed on this website have been selected by the City of West Sacramento as locations where farming would benefit the community. Additionally, any property owned by the city can be leased for a period of at least five years at a rate of $1 per year. For additional information, or to find out how to list a property that you own, please contact Aaron Laurel, Development Coordinator for the City of West Sacramento.
